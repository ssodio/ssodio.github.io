About Me

Hiya! My name is Sulphine Susie Odio, and I am a junior transfer student pursuing computer science at the University of California, Berkeley. I grew up in the east side of San Jose, CA, where I was surrounded by supportive family members, good food, and innovative tech companies!

I gravitate towards all-things-art, adore my STEM classes, and love seeing people with similar interests come together. Whether it was designing for my high school's yearbook, working as a Front-End Web Development Intern at Infinity Enterprises, or supporting my peers in the Women in Computer Science Club, I find joy in creative, challenging, and collaborative environments.

The versatility of computer science allows me to combine all of my passions. With that said, I am seeking opportunities in UI/UX Design and Product Design!

Thanks for stopping by! :)
